Guitar:

What to practice:

  • Open Chords (G, C, D, A, E)
  • Spider exercise (Up to 12th fret)
  • A minor pentatonic scale
  • Picking exercises
  • Recognizing chords/notes by ear (Ear training)
  • Songs
  • Learn what chords are what names (Ex. E7, Am, etc.)
