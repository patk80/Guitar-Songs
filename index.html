What to practice:

  • Finger stretching
  • Open Chords (G, C, D, A, E)
  • Chord changes
  • Spider exercise (Up to 12th fret) with a metronome
  • Scales (Ex. A minor penatonic)
  • Picking exercises
  • Recognizing chords/notes by ear (Ear training)
  • Songs
  • Learn what chords are what names (Ex. E7, E minor, Am, etc.)
  • Picking exercises
  • Music theory
  • Improvising with a backing track
  • Hammer-ons and pull-offs
  • Palm muting
  • Song writing?
